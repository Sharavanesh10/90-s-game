                                                   NIM_GAME
About:
       This game is a mathematical game of strategy in which two players take turns removing (or "nimming") objects from distinct heaps or piles. On each turn, a player must remove at least one object, and may remove any number of objects provided they all come from the same heap or pile.


Rules And Regulations:
1).this game is an multiplayer game.
2).Game generate the random number sticks and you should start only with that
3).The player should click the button to remove the sticks.
4).The player can remove minimum 1 stick and maximum 3 sticks per chance.
5). Each player get their chance alternatively.
6).The player who picks the last stick is the losser,and the oppenent player wins the game.
7).Use your own strategy to make the oppenent to take the last stick.
